Plot Overview:

In a post-apocalyptic world, where humanity resides within domed cities, Inspector Re-L Mayer investigates a series of murders committed by self-aware humanoid robots called AutoReivs. Her quest leads her to uncover a conspiracy involving enigmatic beings known as Proxies, who hold the key to understanding the truth about their existence and the devastated Earth. The show delves into philosophical themes and draws inspiration from Gnostic beliefs, creating a visually stunning and thought-provoking experience
