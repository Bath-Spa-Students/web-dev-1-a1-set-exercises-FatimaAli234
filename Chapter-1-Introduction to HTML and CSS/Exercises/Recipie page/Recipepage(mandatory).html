Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken fried rice is a classic dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with diced chicken, vegetables , and eggs in a wok or skillet. Seasoned with soy sauce, oyster sauce, and other spices,it offers a flavorful combination of tender chicken, fluffy rice, and crunchy vegetables, making it a popular choice for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Rice (cooked, or 1/2 cup uncooked rice)
  • add Salt (as needed)
  • 1tablespoon Garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon Soya Sauce (organic, adjust to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon Pepper (crushed, or pepper)
  • 2 tablespoons Oil
  • 2 Green Chilies (optional, or any hot sauce as desired)
  • 2 sprigs Spring Onions (or scallions, or 1 small onion, greens & whites separated)
  • ½ cup Bell Peppers (chopped)
  • 150 grams Chicken (chopped to bite size)
  • 1 Egg (or 3/4 tbsp corn starch or brine, refer notes)
  • ½ teaspoon Soya Sauce
  • 1 large pinch Salt
  • 1 large pinch Pepper (crushed, or powder)

Method:

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions. Allow it to cool.
  2. In a small bowl, beat the egg with soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  3. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the egg mixture and scramble until cooked. Remove from the wok and set aside.
  4. In the same wok, add a little more oil if needed. Stir-fry garlic, green chilies, and white parts of spring onions until fragrant.
  5. Add chicken and stir-fry until cooked through.
  6. Add bell peppers and green parts of spring onions. Cook until slightly softened.
  7. Add cooked rice and stir-fry everything together, breaking up any clumps of rice.
  8. Drizzle with soy sauce and oyster sauce. Stir well to combine.
  9. Return the scrambled eggs to the wok. Stir until everything is heated through.
  10. Adjust seasoning with salt, pepper, or additional soy sauce if needed.
  11. Serve hot, garnished with chopped spring onions if desired.
